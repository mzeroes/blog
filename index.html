My Story

🪄 Where It All Began...

My first experience with coding came during high school. Even without a computer at home, I spent countless hours in the school computer lab during breaks, learning the basics of C++. My first project? A 'Who Wants to Be a Millionaire' game, simple yet incredibly rewarding.

In 10th grade, I attended a hacking workshop at DTU, which ignited my passion for cybersecurity. This marked the beginning of an exciting journey into tech, leading me to pursue a Bachelor's degree in Information Technology from MAIT, New Delhi.

During college, I interned at Grappus, which transitioned into a full-time role as a Full Stack Developer. It was a whirlwind of learning and growth, as I took on various challenging projects and developed strong technical and interpersonal skills.

Currently, I’m working as a Software Engineer at Deutsche Telekom Digital Labs. I’m always eager to explore new opportunities and dive deeper into the world of technology.
